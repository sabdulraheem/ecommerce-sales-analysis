# E-commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

This repository contains a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales and profitability. Interactive HTML charts provide insights into sales trends, profitability, and top-performing products/categories.

---

## **Project Overview**

The project focuses on understanding sales and profit trends across categories, sub-categories, and products. Key objectives include:

- Track monthly and yearly sales and profit growth.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing categories, sub-categories, and products.
- Evaluate profitability with metrics like Profitability Index and Sales-to-Profit Ratio.
- Generate actionable insights to optimize inventory, pricing, and product strategy.

## **Category-Level Analysis**

### Profit by Category
<iframe src="https://sabdulraheem.github.io/ecommerce-sales-analysis/Profit_by_category.html" width="100%" height="500px"></iframe>
**Insight:** Electronics contribute 40% of total profit ($120K), followed by Apparel (~$80K). Office Supplies show high volume but low profit ($15K).  

### Pareto Analysis by Category
<iframe src="https://sabdulraheem.github.io/ecommerce-sales-analysis/Pareto_Category.html" width="100%" height="500px"></iframe>
**Insight:** Top 20% categories contribute 75% of total profit. Electronics and Apparel dominate.  

### Pie Chart of Sales by Category
<iframe src="https://sabdulraheem.github.io/ecommerce-sales-analysis/Piechartofsalesbycategory.html" width="100%" height="500px"></iframe>
**Insight:** Electronics account for 35% of sales, Apparel 25%, and other categories split the remainder.  

### Profitability Index by Category
<iframe src="https://sabdulraheem.github.io/ecommerce-sales-analysis/ProfitabilityIndexbyCategory.html" width="100%" height="500px"></iframe>
